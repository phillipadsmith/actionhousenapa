About the studio

Built by someone who has spent a career telling stories that matter.

Phillip Smith is an industry professional with more than three decades of experience: executive coach, entrepreneurship trainer, former Director of the Google News Initiative Journalistic Creators Lab, Stanford JSK Journalism Fellow, and Mozilla Senior Fellow for Media & Trust. He has personally delivered 500+ hours of one-on-one coaching to video creators, newsroom founders, and media leaders around the world. Phillip started his career as a photojournalist and studio founder, and is excited to bring those creative roots to Napa.

When you record at Action House, you are not just renting a room. You are working with someone who understands why your conversation deserves to be heard, and how to make sure it is.
